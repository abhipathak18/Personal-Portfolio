AI Meeting Summarizer

A team-built tool that transcribes and summarizes meetings automatically. Audio is transcribed with Whisper, speakers are separated with Pyannote.audio for diarization, and the transcript is condensed into a clean summary using GPT‑4o / Gemini — served through a FastAPI backend to a Flutter / React Native app.
